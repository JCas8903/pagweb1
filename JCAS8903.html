Michael Jordan

Biography

Michael Jeffrey Jordan (New York, February 17, 1963) is a former American basketball player. At 1.98 meters tall, he played in the shooting guard position. He is considered by the majority of fans and specialists to be the best basketball player of all time. He retired definitively at the age of forty in 2003 with the Washington Wizards, after having done so on two previous occasions, in 1993 and 1998, after having played 13 seasons for the Chicago Bulls.
